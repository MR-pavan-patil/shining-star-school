About Our School

Where every child's journey begins with love & care

Welcome to Shining Star Public School

Located in the heart of Bidar at Hyder Colony, Chidri Road, Shining Star Public School has been a beacon of quality early childhood education for over a decade.

We believe that every child is unique and deserves a nurturing environment where they can explore, discover, and grow. Our school is designed to be a second home for your little ones — safe, warm, and filled with joy.

With experienced, caring teachers and a curriculum that balances academics with creative activities, we ensure that your child's formative years are the best they can be.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and loved preschool in Bidar, nurturing every child's potential through a joyful, safe, and stimulating learning environment that prepares them for a bright future.

Our Mission

To provide a warm, child-centered environment where little ones develop confidence, creativity, and a love for learning through play-based education, guided by experienced and caring teachers.

PRINCIPAL

Mrs. Rajeshree M. Bhalke

"Every child deserves an education that unlocks their full potential."

Our principal, Mrs. Rajeshree M. Bhalke, is a dedicated and passionate educator with a vision to provide quality education to all. With 24 years of experience in teaching and administration, she has been instrumental in shaping the academic journey of our students.

Under her leadership, Shining Star Public School has grown into a reputable institution, known for its academic excellence and holistic approach to education. Her commitment to education is evident in the school's curriculum, which emphasises not only academic achievement but also character development and extracurricular activities.

Mrs. Bhalke is a strong advocate for student-centered learning and believes in empowering students to take ownership of their education. She is always available to support students, parents, and teachers, and her door is always open to new ideas and suggestions.

24+ Years Experience Student-Centered Learning Holistic Education

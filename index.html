About Our School

Located in the heart of Bidar, Shining Star Public School has been a beacon of quality early childhood education. We believe that every child is unique and deserves a nurturing environment where they can explore, discover, and grow.

With experienced, caring teachers and a curriculum that balances academics with creative activities, we ensure that your child's formative years are filled with joy and learning.
